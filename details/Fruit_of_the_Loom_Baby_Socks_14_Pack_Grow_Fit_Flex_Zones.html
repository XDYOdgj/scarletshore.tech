Fruit of the Loom 14-Pack Baby Socks

These Fruit of the Loom 14-pack baby socks are built to grow with your little one, featuring GROW & FIT ZONES that offer extra stretch and comfort as they wiggle and develop. The ribbed areas are specially designed to provide flexibility without compromising softness, making them perfect for active babies who are constantly moving. With non-skid grippers on the soles, they add an extra layer of security, helping to prevent slips during play or learning new skills.

The soft elastic rib knit cuff is gentle on delicate skin, ensuring easy on/off and all-day wear comfort. Whether your baby is at home, in the crib, or out exploring, these socks are designed to keep them cozy and safe. The inclusion of size indicators on each pair makes it simple to sort and match, especially when doing laundry.

Crafted from a premium material blend of 63% cotton, 33% polyester, 2% spandex, and 2% rubber, these socks are durable yet breathable. They are ideal for everyday wear and offer a stylish look that complements any outfit. The growing size range up to 24 months means these socks fit longer, reducing the need for frequent replacements and helping you save money over time.
