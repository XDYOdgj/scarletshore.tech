Carhartt Women's Rain Defender Lightweight Coat

This stylish yet practical raincoat is designed for women who demand reliable protection against the elements. Built with Carhartt's innovative Wind Fighter technology, it effectively blocks out wind and keeps you dry, even in unpredictable weather. The relaxed fit ensures all-day comfort, making it perfect for casual wear or more rugged activities. Whether you're running errands, exploring the outdoors, or simply need a dependable outerwear piece, the Rain Defender delivers both function and fashion.

  • Wind Fighter technology for superior wind resistance
