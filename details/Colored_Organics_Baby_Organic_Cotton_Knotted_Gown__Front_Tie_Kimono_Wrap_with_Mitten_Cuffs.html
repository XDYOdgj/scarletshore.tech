Adorable Organic Cotton Baby Knotted Gown

A gentle, soft, and stylish choice for your little one, crafted with care and sustainability in mind.

Soft and Hypoallergenic Comfort

This baby gown is made from 100% organic cotton, which is naturally soft and gentle on your baby's delicate skin. The fabric is free from harmful chemicals, making it hypoallergenic and perfect for sensitive skin.
