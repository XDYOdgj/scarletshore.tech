ShinyMatch High Top Football Socks

These high top sports socks are designed to keep you moving in comfort and confidence, no matter the sport or the intensity of play.

With a unique 3.5-inch elasticated opening, ShinyMatch High Top Football Socks deliver a secure fit that prevents slippage, even during the most active games. This feature ensures your socks stay in place, allowing you to focus on your performance without distractions.

The extra long design extends above the knee, providing full leg coverage that's perfect for baseball, football, and other sports. It adds an extra layer of support and protection, keeping your legs warm and your athletic wear looking sharp.

Constructed from a durable blend of 66% polypropylene, 25% polyester, 7% high stretch tonal rubber, and 2% spandex, these sports socks are built to last. The breathable fabric prevents moisture buildup, keeping your feet dry and reducing the risk of blisters throughout your session.

Whether you're in the gym, on the field, or playing baseball, these moisture wicking sports socks are the perfect match for your gear. Their seamless construction ensures a smooth fit, while the versatility in sizes and colors makes them suitable for every athlete and every occasion.

ShinyMatch combines functionality with a stylish look, making these athletic socks a must-have for players who want both performance and appearance. Ideal for men and women, these high top sports socks are a great addition to your sports wardrobe for all-day wear comfort and support.

Key Features

  • Elasticated opening prevents slipping during intense activity
  • Extra long design covers above the knee for full leg support
  • Durable blend of polypropylene, polyester, and spandex
  • Breathable and moisture-wicking fabric keeps feet dry
  • Available in multiple sizes and colors for versatile fit
